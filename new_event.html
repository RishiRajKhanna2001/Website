Kalopsia

Vent Along Hues

Workshop Series

January, 2022

Kalopsia will hit you with a double blast; it will let you instil beautiful panoramas on stones, through STONE ART and stitch your favourite characters alive, through MANIKIN-MAKING.

The event, a two Sunday affair is an offline event (will also be streamed online) which will take place on the following dates:

9th January 2022– Stone Art
16th January 2022– Manikin Making

VENUE– Silveria Hall, SGSITS Campus, Indore
TIME– 3:00 PM to 6:00 PM
