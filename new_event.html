Meraki:The Musical Mosaic.

Event

August, 2021

Meraki will let your creativity tap to rhythm. Participants will have to use their artistry in the form of artworks (handmade/digital) or photographs which relate to a song. Just like eyes are windows to the soul, your submission shall exhibit lyrics. Bubbling with interpretation and imagination, cover arts are the pillars of a song’s existence.

Rules

For smooth execution of the event , the organizers have agreed upon the following guidelines:

1. This is purely an art and photography based event.

2. Participants have to choose a suitable song of their choice and have to provide us a particular verse of it.

3. On the basis of the selection of song , the participants have to create an artwork or a digital art or click a photograph.

4. The maximum number of submissions is 2 per participant.

5. Time limit for submission will be upto 48 hrs that is 27-29 August.

6. Winners will be decided by instagram story polling. The highest number of votes bagger will win the contest. There will be first and second runner up as well.

Rules

7. Those artworks that follow all the guidelines will be featured on our stories.

8. Instructions related to song selection:

  • Participants are free to choose songs of their choice.
  • There is no language barrier.
  • Any kind of inappropriate conntent will lead to disqualification.
  • It is strictly required by the participant to only send a particular verse(lyrics) of the song and not the whole of it , this can be done by selecting a particular set of lyrics within the song.

    • 9. Any type of plagaiarism will not be entertained.

  • Polling mode: instagram
